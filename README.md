# deep_drive-analysis-interactive-dashboarding
To answer complex,multi-faceted business problems by performing a deep-dive analysis and buliding an interactive,automated dashboard

Task 3 – Deep-Dive Analysis & Interactive Dashboarding (Power BI)
Overview
This project focuses on customer transaction data. Using Power BI, the dashboard provides KPI cards, cohort analysis, and interactive visuals to understand revenue performance and customer retention.
1. Dataset
Columns:
TransactionID
CustomerID
Name
DOB
Gender
Product
Quantity
Price
PurchaseDate
Age
Revenue

Key Metrics:
Total Revenue: 1,041,600
Average Revenue per Transaction: 54,821.05

2. Dashboard Features
KPI Cards
Total Revenue
Average Order Value
Retention Rate
Top Product Revenue
Total Transactions
Cohort Matrix
Retention trends by cohort month
Customer retention analysis over time

3. Supporting Visuals
Product vs Revenue (Bar Chart)
Displays revenue generated by each product category and identifies top-performing products.
Gender vs Revenue (Pie Chart)
Shows revenue contribution by male and female customers.
Revenue Trend (Line Chart)
Tracks revenue performance over time and highlights growth patterns.
Interactive Slicers
Product
Gender
Purchase Date
These slicers allow users to dynamically filter dashboard insights.

4. Key Insights
Laptop generated the highest revenue (approximately 770K).
Male customers contributed approximately 62% of total revenue, while female customers contributed approximately 38%.
Customer retention begins at 100% during the first cohort month.
Average Order Value is approximately 96.2K.

Conclusion
This dashboard combines deep-dive cohort analysis with interactive visualizations to provide a comprehensive view of customer retention and revenue performance. It helps identify top-performing products, customer segments, and retention patterns, making it a valuable business intelligence solution for data-driven decision-making.
